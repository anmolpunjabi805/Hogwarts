Courses

Each year at Hogwarts is a chapter in your magical journey, filled with knowledge, challenges, friendships, and growth. Make the most of it, for Hogwarts will always be home!

Year Pedagogy
1st year Welcome to Hogwarts, young witches and wizards! Your journey begins with the awe-inspiring entrance into the wizarding world. Get sorted into your house by the Sorting Hat, make new friends, attend the Sorting Ceremony, and start your magical studies in various subjects like Potions, Charms, and Transfiguration.
2nd year Delve deeper into the magical world as Hogwarts reveals more of its secrets. Encounter challenges, expand your knowledge in classes like Herbology and Defense Against the Dark Arts, and explore the castle's hidden passages and magical creatures.
3rd year The castle grounds expand with the introduction of Care of Magical Creatures and the unraveling of more advanced magical theories. Prepare for the challenges ahead as you navigate through subjects like Divination and learn about the history of the wizarding world.
4th year The Triwizard Tournament captivates the school! Though only accessible to older students, excitement fills the air as Hogwarts hosts this legendary event. Witness daring competitions, expand your magical skills, and experience the thrill of international wizarding unity.
5th year O.W.L.s approach! These crucial exams determine your academic future and the courses you'll take in the final years.Buckle down for intense studies as you prepare for these assessments in various magical disciplines.
6th year N.E.W.T.s loom on the horizon. Focused studies in specialized subjects pave the way for your future career in the magical world. Dive deeper into practical magic and explore the subjects that ignite your passion.
7th year The final chapter at Hogwarts! Strengthen friendships, make lasting memories, and prepare for life beyond the castle walls. Seize every magical moment as you embrace the responsibilities and adventures that await in the wider wizarding community.
Courses Description
1. Potions Involves the brewing of magical concoctions with various effects.
2. Transfiguration Teaches the art of changing the form or appearance of an object.
3. Charms Focuses on spells and incantations for specific purposes, like levitation or unlocking doors.
4. Defense Against the Dark Arts Covers defensive spells and counter-curses against dark magic.
5. Herbology Studies magical plants and their magical properties.
6. Divination Involves predicting the future using various methods,
7. Care of Magical Creatures Deals with the care and understanding of magical animals.
8. Astronomy Studies celestial bodies and their magical significance.
9. History of Magic Explores the history of the wizarding world.
10. Arithmancy Involves magical properties of numbers and their use in spellcasting.

Additionally, there might be elective courses, specialized studies for certain careers (like Auror training), and other subjects that vary year to year.

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry offers a variety of magical courses that students attend to learn and develop their magical abilities.
